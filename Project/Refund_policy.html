REFUND POLICY:

Development-cart does not offer refunds for services rendered. Once payment has been made, it is non-refundable. Company believes in providing high-quality services and will work diligently to ensure your satisfaction. If you have any concerns or issues with the services rendered, please contact us at info.Development-cart@gmail.com immediately, and we will work to resolve them as soon as possible.

CANCELLATION POLICY:

Development-cart understands that sometimes circumstances change, and you may need to cancel your service. We require a minimum of 48 hours notice prior to the scheduled start of the service for cancellations. If you cancel within 48 hours of the scheduled start time, you will be charged a cancellation fee of 50% of the total service cost. If you cancel after the service has started, you will be charged the full service cost. In the event that our agency needs to cancel a scheduled service, we will provide a full refund or reschedule the service at your convenience.

Please note that this policy is subject to change at any time, and we will notify you of any updates or changes as soon as possible. If you have any questions or concerns, please feel free to contact us at info.Development-cart@gmail.com
